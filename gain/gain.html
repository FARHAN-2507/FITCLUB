Gain Weight

Introduction

Weight gain can be just as challenging as weight loss for many individuals. However, gaining weight in a healthy and sustainable manner is important for those looking to build muscle mass, increase strength, and improve overall health.


The key to successful weight gain is to focus on consuming nutrient-rich foods in adequate quantities and engaging in regular strength training exercises. By following a well-balanced diet and exercise plan, you can achieve your weight gain goals effectively.

Diet Plans

Plan 1

TOTAL CALORIES

Breakfast

Food: Paratha (Indian flatbread) with paneer (cottage cheese) curry

Recipe: Whole wheat paratha stuffed with paneer cooked in a tomato-onion gravy

Additional: Serve with a side of yogurt and sliced fruits


Mid-Morning Snack

Food: Almond milkshake with banana and dates

Recipe: Blend almond milk with ripe bananas, dates, and a touch of honey

Additional: Add a scoop of protein powder for extra calories


Lunch

Food: Rajma (kidney bean) curry with rice

Recipe: Rajma cooked in a spiced tomato-onion gravy, served with basmati rice

Additional: Include a side of mixed vegetable salad dressed with lemon juice


Afternoon Snack

Food: Dhokla (steamed savory cake) with mint chutney

Recipe: Soft and fluffy dhokla made from fermented rice and chickpea flour, served with mint chutney

Additional: Garnish with grated coconut and chopped cilantro


Dinner

Food: Chicken biryani

Recipe: Fragrant basmati rice cooked with marinated chicken, spices, and caramelized onions

Additional: Serve with cucumber raita (yogurt dip) and boiled eggs
